Welcome to the HIDE Lab!

The Hybrid Intelligence and Deception Exploration (HIDE) Lab focuses on understanding the complexity of human-AI interactions and deception in increasingly hybrid societies. The lab is led by Dr. Stefan Sarkadi, who is currently a Proleptic Lecturer (Research Assistant Professor) in the Dept. of Informatics at King's College London. The Lab's aim is to act as an international hub for conducting state-of-the-art AI-based research to tackle the topic of deception.
